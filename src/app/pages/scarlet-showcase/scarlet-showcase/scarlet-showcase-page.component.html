What is Scarlet Showcase?

Scarlet Showcase is a showcase of the student work from creative clubs at Rutgers! Creation of Games Society (COGS), Animation Club (AC), Japanese Visual Culture Association (JVCA), Voice Acting Network and Collective of Entertainers (VANCE), and Rutgers University Club for Video Game Music (RUCVGM) are coming together to showcase member projects and run fun activities!

Come try out video games from COGS, listen to music made by RUCVGM, voice act in a mini recording booth run by VANCE, [] from JVCA, and view the rough cut of Animation Club's upcoming animated short film! See the Itinerary below for a full list of activities.

May 4th, 3 - 7 PM Livingston Hall

Scarlet Showcase will be hosted inside Livingston Hall, which is in the Livingston Student Center. The event will run from 3 pm to 7 pm. A Google Map view of the location is shown for your convenience.

RSVP for the event on this event's GetInvolved page, and join the COGS discord for more information!

Showcase + Activities

Explore engaging activities and exhibits hosted by some of Rutgers' digital creative clubs. If you participate in an activity from every club, you're eligible for a steam key raffle! Here's a list of all the activities and showcases:

COGS

  • Demoing COGS member semesterly video game projects
  • RuckusWare Tournament
    • Compete in a tournament in our own in-house microgame collection, RuckusWare.
    • 8 entrant slots.
    • Entrants will be found prior to event.
  • Merch
    • Buy video game-themed merchnadise like stickers to help support COGS!

RUCVGM

  • Showcasing member performances and projects
  • Guess that Song game music quiz
  • Live performance!

VANCE

  • Showcase of VANCE projects
  • Live script reading and dubbing
    • Spin a giant spinner to select a random short voice-acting script, and deliver your best take into the mic!

Animation Club

    • At their table on loop

7:00 PM

Awards Ceremony

Join us for an opportunity to celebrate the achievements of each club and their outstanding members. Presidents from each club will take to the stage to recognize the dedication and creativity of their peers, as well as the exceptional work done by their club as a whole. Various awards and trophies will be presented to club members, with the trophies generously provided by RUMakers. Attendees can vote on a few community awards, so be sure to vote on your favorite projects. You won't want to miss this inspirational conclusion to the Scarlet Showcase!
